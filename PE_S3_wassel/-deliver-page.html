BECOME A DRIVER

And become our partner, Start earing by delivering orders at you own chosen time and area





WHAT YOU WILL DO AS A DRIVER

The driver's role involves delivering orders from the shop to the customer's house. Upon receiving delivery requests, the driver accepts the delivery and obtains the pickup and drop-off addresses. Subsequently, they collect the order, deliver it to the specified location, and receive payment after confirming delivery by scanning a QR code provided by the customer





WHY BECOME A DRIVER


FLEXIBLE WORKTIME

Work on your terms—choose your hours, any day, anytime. Whether you're at home or away, you can log in from anywhere and handle deliveries on your schedule.

EASY USAGE

We ensure every user enjoys a hassle-free app experience—from easily discovering orders to tracking and confirming them effortlessly.

INTEGRITY

We prioritize prompt payment upon delivery for our drivers through an efficient order tracking and confirmation system. Moreover, we offer bonuses for dedicated and passionate drivers to encourage continuity and commitment.
